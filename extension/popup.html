Edit the weights (Wt.) of cell values, rows, and columns in the policy grade's formula.
Each cell is given a score, which is its value's weight multiplied by the weight of its row and the weight of its column. The sum of all cells' scores is the policy's score.
The grade is the decided by how big the policy's score is compared to the maximal policy score (= the score of a table that's filled with the maximal-weight cell value)

All privacy policy summaries from the server are cached. When they expire, they move to long-term storage to detect policy updates on the next time you visit their website.
If a website updates its policy, its updated summary will only be seen after its old cached summary expires. Expire the cache to load up-to-date summaries for all websites .

Hi there!
We're a group of 3 computer science students from Tel-Aviv University. This extension is our project for a workshop class on usable security and privacy.
We were tasked with using OpenAI's GPT models to summarize websites' privacy policies and make as accessible as possible to the average internet user.
Our goal is to raise the users' awareness to website's data collection practices, and possibly change their browsing habits.
